Armors

Defense is everything in this game. The damage the mosters deal can rip you to shreds if you are not wearing the right suit of armor. Armor affects two things: your combat defenses and elemental modifiers. They provide the base which things like shields then add to. Below is a list of armors in alphabetical order.

  • Breakneck Robe - Neutral
  • Dark Plate - Darkness
  • Earthen Plate - Earth
  • Elite Frogzard Hunter - Earth
  • Energy Plate - Energy
  • Fire Plate - Fire
  • Flower Armor - Water
  • Frogzard Hunter - Earth
  • Frogzard Rider Armor - Neutral
  • Frogzilla Armor - Earth
  • Golden Plate - Neutral
  • Ice Plate - Ice
  • Invisibility Cloak - Wind
  • Pumpkin Armor - Water
  • Salamander Rider - Fire
  • Water Plate - Water
  • Werewolf Form - Earth
  • Wind Plate - Wind
  • Zombie Form - Neutral
