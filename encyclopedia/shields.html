Shields

Shields are no replacement for a suit of armor by any means. What they do is modify the base set up by the armor and make your defenses better. With the shields you will see a "+" and "-" beside each stat to indicate how they change your defense. Although there aren't many good ones, there are a couple that will last you a long time.

  • Dimensional - Neutral
  • Energy Shield - Energy
  • Ethos - Neutral
  • Fire Shield - Fire
  • Frogzard Shield - Neutral
  • Golden - Neutral
  • Ice Shield - Ice
  • Logos - Neutral
  • Monster Claw - Neutral
  • Pathos - Neutral
  • Shield of Darkness - Darkness
  • Water Shield - Water
  • Wind Shield - Wind
  • Wooden Shield - Earth
